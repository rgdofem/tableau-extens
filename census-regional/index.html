Measure roles
Drop measures on the Measures tile, then reopen this dialog to assign roles. Until then, roles are auto-detected from field names.
Measures not assigned to a role become extra crosstab columns (payor %, ALOS, …). Without a Prior Year measure, PY is derived from the Date history — keep last year's dates in the worksheet if you want it.

Budget grain
Only used when de-duplicating. If a monthly-per-location budget is repeated across a finer row dimension (e.g. Program), summing it overcounts. Check the dimension(s) the budget is actually unique by — e.g. Location — and the KPIs, variance chart and crosstab totals take one value per group instead of summing. With Rows at exactly the budget's grain, leave this on "Add it up".
